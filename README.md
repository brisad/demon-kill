# demon-kill

A very simple hangman-like game.

Simple but epic gameplay:

```
Welcome to demon-kill!
 @ <- you              demon -> &

___________
Enter your guess: a
 @  the demon is coming! &

___________
Enter your guess: u
 @               &

___________
Enter your guess: e
 @               &

_____e____e
Enter your guess: p
 @               &

p____e____e
Enter your guess: l
 @       &

p____e____e
Enter your guess:
```
